About Me

Hi, I'm Sarah, a Physiotherapist dedicated to personalized care. After qualifying in 2017, I spent three impactful years specializing in respiratory physiotherapy at the NHS before launching Full Motion Physiotherapy during my maternity leave. Recently rebranded as Sarah Ayling Physiotherapy, I aim to provide a more personal touch to patient care.

Beyond my work, I'm passionate about staying active through running and weightlifting, emphasizing the importance of exercise in enhancing lifestyles. With a background in diverse patient care both in NHS and private settings, I find joy in assisting individuals, no matter their goals.

My focus is now on pelvic and women's health within physiotherapy, driven by a strong commitment to supporting women's often overlooked health needs. If you have questions about how I can help you, let's connect and explore the possibilities for your well-being.

My Services

I offer a range of services to help you recover and feel your best.

Inital Assesment - 45 minutes

As a new patient, we'll start by thoroughly evaluating your injury or condition. We'll begin by discussing your medical history and the specifics of your condition to tailor our approach.

Once we've gathered this information, we'll conduct a detailed examination of your injury or condition. Together, we'll collaboratively develop a treatment plan that suits your needs and goals.

At the conclusion of the assessment, I'll provide you with a diagnosis and a comprehensive explanation of your condition. I'll also create a plan for your next steps, which may involve follow-up treatment sessions or recommendations for self-care exercises at home.

Follow-up - 30 minutes

During your follow-up appointment, we'll review your progress since your initial assessment or last visit. This discussion covers the evolution of your injury or condition, reflecting on your experiences following previous appointments, including treatment outcomes, successes, and challenges encountered.

Moreover, I'll conduct a comprehensive re-evaluation of your injury or condition, comparing it to your prior assessment. This helps us measure the effectiveness of the interventions and identify any improvements or regressions.

Drawing from our discussion and re-evaluation, we'll collaboratively assess your treatment plan. Together, we'll decide on potential adjustments to ensure it remains aligned with your evolving needs and goals.

Extended follow-up - 45 minutes

In an extended 45-minute follow-up session, we'll thoroughly discuss your progress since the initial assessment or last appointment, covering the current status of your injury or condition, along with your experiences in following the treatment plan.

We'll then conduct a detailed re-evaluation of your injury or condition, comparing it to the prior assessment. This helps us gauge the effectiveness of the interventions and track any changes that have occurred.

Building on our discussion and re-evaluation, we'll collaboratively review your treatment plan. We'll assess if adjustments are needed to ensure it aligns with your evolving needs and goals. This extended session provides the time for a more personalized approach, allowing us to optimize your progress and outcomes.

Home Visits

Physiotherapy services at the comfort of your home for those encountering difficulties reaching a clinic. If traveling to a clinic poses challenges for you or someone you know, I offer convenient home visit appointments for initial assessments and ongoing treatments.

This option is especially advantageous for functional assessments and rehabilitation tailored to address challenges within your home environment.

By conducting assessments in the familiar surroundings of your home, I can better tailor rehabilitation strategies that consider your daily activities, living arrangements, and any specific challenges you may face. This comprehensive approach ensures that the physiotherapy interventions provided are not only effective but also seamlessly integrated into your daily life, fostering long-term wellness and recovery.
